About Online Shopping

Online shopping is a form of electronic commerce which allows consumers to directly buy goods or services from a seller over the Internet. It enables customers to browse a range of products, compare prices, and make secure purchases.

Customers can use desktops, laptops, tablets, or smartphones to shop online. The convenience of shopping from anywhere, at any time, has made online shopping increasingly popular.
